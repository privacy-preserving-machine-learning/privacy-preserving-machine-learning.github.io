  • Principal Investigator

    •   Mijung Park is an assistant professor in the department of Computer Science at the University of British Columbia. She i also a CIFAR AI Chair at Amii. Prior to this, she was a research group leader at the MPI-IS Tuebingen and a Cyber Valley research group leader at the university of Tuebingen. Prior to that, she was a postdoc at the informatics institute at UvA (with Max Welling), and at the Gatsby Unit at UCL (with Maneesh Sahani). She received a Ph.D. at UT Austin (supervised by Jonathan Pillow). Her CV is available here.
